CEO Maria Johnson started Firstclass Interiors in 2005 after travelling to Bali, Phuket, Thailand and the Pacific Islands such as Samoa, Fiji, Taihiti and Hawai'i. She fell in love with the tropical way of life and as a designer wanted to bring that love of the tropical into her work. She holds her Bachelor of Architecture from the University of Auckland with a Postgraduate Diploma in Interior Design and Business Studies, perfect for bringing to life her design visions for all your Tropical design needs.



Managing Director, Alia Bhatt is an accomplished actor and singer in Bollywood film. She also has her own eco company and her own line of handbags. Her business acumen sets her up well as the CEO's second in command of Firstclass Interiors. Her experience in India brings with her the sense of the Tropics of the Indian Ocean, namely Goa, the Seychelles and Maldives.



Recently appointed Lead Designer Taika Waititi heads our design team. He comes with a wealth of experience in the film and theatre industry, hotbed of creativity. He studied theatre at Victoria University of Wellington where he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in 1997. He brings with him his theatre design skills and wonderful sense of the Tropics from New Zealand and the Pacific Islands.



Our Second Lead Designer is Erin Gray. Coming from a modelling and acting background and running her own business for many years, she brings a wealth of design skills along with her business and budgeting skills. Born in Hawai'i she has a deep sense of the Tropical spirit which she shares with her clients while remaining culturally sensitive.



Rhys Darby is our graphic designer extraordinaire. Gaining his Masters Degree in Graphic Design from the National Design School and then his Postgraduate Diploma in Interior Design, he brings with him a keen eye for detail and colour blending. He creates mockups of your designs digitally before the work starts so that you can have an idea right from the start what the completed design will look like and easily make changes along the way until it is perfect.



Lily Fong is our fabrics and furnishings designer. She has a Bachelor of Fashion Design degree from the National Design School, speacialising in interior furnishing. Her keen eye for furniture placement and colour blending will have a client's space looking like the dream they want. Born on the island of Reunion in the Indian Ocean, and having lived in New Caledonia and Samoa, Lily has a deep understanding of the Tropics and Island culture and shares these authentically in her designs.



Business Manager Roger Nelson comes to us with a wealth of business knowledge in the creative field. Having fronted and managed the band 'Prince' for many years he has honed his business skills. He understands the business and administrative management of a creative endeavour and keeps our agency running like the tight, successful ship that it is in order for us to realise our clients' design dreams.
